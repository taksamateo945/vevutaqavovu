Did Jessie James Decker Get Plastic Surgery?

But perhaps what has intrigued fans the most recently is her decision to undergo plastic surgery.

In May 2021, Jessie shared on Instagram that she opted for breast augmentation surgery, citing her conclusion that her family was complete.

Previously, she had undergone breast reduction and lift surgery a couple of years back but wasn’t satisfied with the results – size and skin laxity.

She aimed to restore the fuller, more youthful appearance she had before motherhood.

Jessie expressed her newfound confidence and happiness with her choice, emphasizing that it was a personal decision she made for herself.

Who is Jessie James Decker?

Born on April 12, 1988, in Vicenza, Italy, while her father was in the US Air Force, Jessie’s childhood was marked by frequent moves, from Louisiana to Georgia to Texas.

She was a singing prodigy, starting at the tender age of two, and penned her first song at just nine.

Her musical influences stemmed from legends like Dolly Parton, Patsy Cline, and Tammy Wynette. Jessie’s journey in the music industry began when she signed her first record deal at the age of 15, and her career blossomed further when she inked a deal with Mercury Records in 2009.

Jessie James Decker’s Career 

Jessie James Decker released her debut album, “Jessie James,” in 2009, which featured the hit single “Wanted,” reaching number 40 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Her talents even extended to songwriting, as she co-wrote “Blue Jeans” for Miley Cyrus’s album “Hannah Montana 2: Meet Miley Cyrus.”

She continued to make waves with her music, releasing the EP “Comin’ Home” in 2011, which included a duet with her husband Eric Decker called “Clint Eastwood.”

In 2014, her EP “Gold” featured the single “Lights Down Low.” Her second studio album, “Southern Girl City Lights,” released in 2017, debuted at number one on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart.

Not just confined to music, she authored her first book, “Just Jessie: My Guide to Love, Life, Family, and Food,” in 2018.

The following year, she ventured into the Christmas-themed album genre with “On This Holiday,” and in 2020, she launched her fashion line, “Kittenish.”

Her star power reached new heights when she joined the cast of “Dancing with the Stars” for its 30th season in 2021.

Jessie James Decker’s Personal Life

Jessie’s personal life has been equally remarkable.

She met Eric Decker in 2011, thanks to a mutual friend who orchestrated a blind date.

The sparks flew, and despite dating long-distance, their connection grew stronger. At the time, Eric was a wide receiver for the Denver Broncos.

Their love story led to an engagement in April 2012 and a wedding in June 2013 in Colorado.

Their family grew with the arrival of Vivianne Rose Decker in March 2014, followed by Eric Thomas Decker II in September 2015 and Forrest Bradley Decker in March 2018.

Eric Decker’s retirement from football in 2018 saw him embrace the role of a stay-at-home dad while wholeheartedly supporting his wife’s career and business ventures.

The couple often graces social media with their affectionate posts, endearing themselves to fans.
