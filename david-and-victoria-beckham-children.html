David and Victoria Beckham’s kids. Cuteness overloaded

Who knew the name ‘Beckham’ before David Beckham came on board? He is one of the greatest football players still living. Consistently, he ranked among the highest earners and most marketable sportsmen. Now as fame would have it, when it was time for a relationship; fame attracted itself. As a young footballer, way back in 1997, David found love with Victoria Adams and they eventually got married on July 4, 1999. Posh Spice as she is popularly called is one of the most stylish women on planet.

For two individuals as famous as David and Victoria Beckham, it is no surprise that the media is drawn to everything and anything they do so much so that their union is dubbed “Posh and Becks”. These two who have been married for years now and have four children. Let’s see what becomes of cute children born into such goodness…

David and Victoria Beckham children: 3 sons and a daughter

Brooklyn Joseph Beckham (son)

Date of Birth: March 4, 1999

Zodiac Sign: Pisces

Born in Westminster, UK, he is the first child of the Beckhams. At first we wondered why that name, but later discovered it was a pointer to where he was conceived in the US. We saw Brooklyn first at the wedding of his parents where he was the ring bearer.

His first TV appearance was in the 2003 documentary “The Real Beckhams” as little as age 3.

Brooklyn Joseph being the first son, we assumed he would follow his dad’s footsteps in the football world but rather he is an up and coming fashion photographer and just shot his first campaign for a Burberry fragrance.

In October 2015, Brooklyn appeared on Miss Vogue as the cover of their October edition, this was his first cover anyways but it was obviously a beautiful first attempt.

The first Beckham son began dating Chloe Moretz in July 2014 but they split saying she’s too clingy.

Just recently, he was the cover of Korea’s Dazed Magazine.


Romeo James Beckham (son)

Date of Birth: September 1, 2002

Zodiac Sign: Virgo

He was born through CS in Portland Hospital, London. Romeo is the second son of the Beckhams. As always, the media could not wait for the name of this baby, thinking it might also reveal where he was conceived.

Seems like the Beckham Kids love Burberry as Romeo once modelled in a video for their winter line. Like his brother Brooklyn, he has fallen in love with fashion and modelling.

Romeo is such a loving child ‘cause he recently rose up to defend his mum when she faced criticism online for kissing his baby sister on the lips. Which parent would not be proud of this? It only goes to show the Beckhams are not only raising their kids in the fashion world but also teaching them good morals.

Cruz David Beckham (son)

Date of Birth: February 20, 2005

Zodiac Sign: Pisces

He was born in Ruber Internacional Hospital in Madrid. With elder brothers showing interest in fashion and modelling, Cruz has brought a new sided to the family name. He has decided to follow his mum’s steps in music.

Cruz’s first public appearance was when he broke out dancing as a toddler at the 2009 Spice Girls’ tour. Aside his incredible ability to dance, Cruz David has a good voice and he already took the first step toward a career in music. People say he might just be the next Justin Bieber.

Harper Seven Beckham (daughter)

Date of Birth: July 10, 2011

Zodiac Sign: Cancer

Okay, she is the youngest child but being the first and only female Beckham, all eyes are on her. As daughters from economically average families, especially the first daughters, our mothers dote on us and make us look as beautiful as possible. Why would you think any different concerning the first female offspring of Posh and Becks.

Her mother is not just a fashion icon, her father is too and they have all the wealth to go with their high stylish taste. She already has her own New York Times style profile because all the fashion labels definitely adore her.

Take a look at Prince George, the Brangelina Kids, Blue Ivy, North West and all other daughters of super celebrities and you’ll see that their parents make it a point of duty to make their little ones beautiful fashion statements. It’s therefore not different with the incredibly chic little Beckham Ballerina who is always seen unfazed by the fame.

Asides fashion and looking chic, Harper is already skilled as a future runway athlete. She seems to have mastered running in heels. Assuredly, David would be really proud of his only daughter who kind of prefers the sports world. She also takes ballet and horse riding class.

They could in no time be the next family as popular as the Kardashian family; their mom thinks they are the next Von Trapp family. All four kids of the Beckhams are definitely on the brink of stardom! This wealthy brood of Beckhams are the faces of the world’s fashion come next decade.
