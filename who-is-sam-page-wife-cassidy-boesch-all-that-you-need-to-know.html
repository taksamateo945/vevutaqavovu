Cassidy Boesch Early Life And Parents: Brought Up In Los Angeles

She was born on 27 August 1987 in California as Cassidy Kateb Boesch. She is the youngest child of Phil and Vivian Boesch. Her parents raised her in Los Angeles with her older brother Brennan Boesch.

Cassidy is of Caucasian and German descent. She grew up in an upper-middle-class family.

Her father is a lawyer by profession and runs The Boesch Law Group in Santa Monica, California. He founded the firm in 2000 and it is one of the leading litigation firms in Los Angeles. Her mother, Viviana, runs The Venice Beach House, a boutique-style hotel.

Given her parents’ professions, it is safe to say that Cassidy lived a pretty luxurious life.

Details On Cassidy Boesch’s Education

Growing up in Los Angeles, Cassidy enrolled at Crossroads School. After graduating from high school, she attended Brown University. She graduated from college with a degree in biomedical public health.

During her studies, Cassidy began working for the welfare of low-income infants and toddlers in Providence, Rhode Island. She then spent three summers in South Africa, Kenya, and Tanzania where she helped implement health and wellness programs at various orphanages.

Upon her return, she enrolled in the University of Southern California’s graduate program and earned a post-graduate degree in Marriage, Family, and Child Therapy.
