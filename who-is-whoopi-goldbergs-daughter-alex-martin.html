WHOOPI Goldberg is well known as a veteran in the entertainment industry.

Similarly, her daughter Alex Martin has followed in her footsteps.

Who is Whoopi Goldberg’s daughter, Alex Martin?

Alex Martin was born on May 9, 1973, in the United States.

Alex, 49, is an actress and film producer.

She is known for her role in Sister Act 2: Back in the Habit, Quest of the Delta Knights, American Intellectuals, Call Me Claus, Strange as Angels, Descendants, and Sensitive Men.

As a result of her stellar work, she was awarded the title of Miss Golden Globe at the 1994 Golden Globe Awards.

Alex is the only child of Whoopi Goldberg and her first husband, Alvin Martin.

Is Alex Martin Married?

Alex Martin is currently married to Bernard Dean.

They tied the knot in 2011.

Currently, they have children together – Amarah Dean, Jerzey Dean, and Mason Dean.

What is Alex Martin’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Alex Martin has an estimated net worth of approximately $4million, as of July 2022.

Her main source of wealth is said to be her career as an actress and producer.

She also earns income from brand endorsements and sponsorships.
