About

Kimiko Glenn is an American actress and singer. She was born on June 27, 1989, in Phoenix, Arizona.

Glenn is best known for her roles as Brook Soso in the Netflix series “Orange Is the New Black” and as Dawn Pinket-Smith in the NBC musical drama series “Zoey’s Extraordinary Playlist”.

She has also appeared in various other TV shows and movies, including “The Guest Book,” “Liza on Demand,” “Nerve,” and “Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse.”

In addition to acting, Glenn is a talented singer and has performed in musical theatre productions.
