5 of Adam Lambert's Best Performances in 2015

July 23, 2024
blog

2015 is coming to an end, and that usually calls for a list of goals, accomplishments, or favorite memories from throughout the year.  What better way to say goodbye to the old year and welcome the new than a list of some of the best Adam Lambert performances!

1.  ‘Ghost Town’ on The Ellen DeGeneres show (4/30)

Starting off the list is Adam’s performance on The Ellen DeGeneres show.  This performance takes our first spot because it was the first time he sang a song (‘Ghost Town’) from his latest album The Original High on TV.  Also, can we take a moment to appreciate the fact that Adam can make an all white outfit look so flawless?

https://youtu.be/bSS6OjTktGc

2. iHeartRadio Album Release Party (6/16)

This performance took place in New York City the day Adam’s album (The Original High) came out. This was streamed online thanks to iHeartRadio, so Glamberts around the world were able to watch! All the songs performed were from The Original High, except ‘Whataya Want From Me‘  from For Your Entertainment.  

There were two major reasons that made this performance so special to us.  One was the fact that we were able to listen to all the new songs live for the first time, and second was the fact that Adam took the time to talk to his fans.  Don’t you agree?

3. Good Morning America (6/19)

During this performance Adam sang ‘Ghost Town’, ‘The Original High’ and ‘Whataya Want From Me.’ He even sang an extra song (‘Evil In The Night’) which didn’t air live, but you can watch it on the second clip below!

https://youtu.be/lMqbMBLLX2c

4. Rock in Rio (9/18)

Rock in Rio was a performance that was also streamed live.  During this set Adam sang with Queen, and they closed the show!  This was the first time Queen performed at the festival since singing with Freddie Mercury back in 1985, 30 years ago.

During this performance, Adam even got to sing a “rock” version of his single ‘Ghost Town’ with Queen.

https://youtu.be/mp2SjLwiJuY?list=PLVB1hnGUslGuRyj9FT6AZ_WA2pztBwMPA

5.  ‘Ghost Town’ on the Today Show (10/7)

Ending our list is Adam’s performance on the Today Show.  Before he sings ‘Ghost Town’ we learn that his latest album The Original High debuted at #3 on the Billboard charts.  Yay Adam!  We also have to admit, he really knows how to work that hat.

https://youtu.be/lTFr1Xj6DQ8
