Khloe Kardashian Is Responsible for Lamar Odom's Medical Decisions

April 21, 2024
blog

Khloe Kardashian is responsible for medical decisions pertaining to her estranged husband, Lamar Odom, who remains in critical condition in a Las Vegas hospital.

Us Weekly revealed on Wednesday, Oct. 14, that Kardashian, 31, and Odom, 35, are still legally married, which means that the reality star, by default, is his primary caretaker. A source confirms to Us that "any medical decisions are up to Khloe" while the divorce documents are still pending.

Though the exes signed off on the papers together this past July, a judge has yet to finalize the divorce. Meanwhile, Kardashian arrived at the Sunrise Hospital in Vegas from L.A. this past Tuesday, immediately after learning about Odom's condition, and hasn't left his side. The former NBA player was found unconscious in a Nevada brothel, where he had checked in on Saturday, Oct. 10.

Another source tells Us that Kardashian has remained hopeful about Odom's condition, despite the circumstances.

"The whole family is hoping he pulls through," the insider tells Us of the Kardashian brood, many of whom have gathered in Vegas and visited Odom upon his transfer. "She will be there hoping he pulls through, but I hear the situation is not very good."

Joining the Kardashians are Odom's ex, Liza Morales, and their two kids, Destiny and Lamar Jr., for whom Khloe made travel arrangements.
