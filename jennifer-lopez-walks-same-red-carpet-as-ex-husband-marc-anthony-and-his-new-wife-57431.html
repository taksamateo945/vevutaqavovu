Friendly Exes!Jennifer Lopez Walks Same Red Carpet as Ex-Husband Marc Anthony and His New Wife

April 10, 2024
blog

Nearly one year after Jennifer Lopez and Marc Anthony finalized their divorce, the former couple reunited at last night’s Billboard Latin Music Awards — and the friendly exes were totally cordial!

At the event, the ‘Booty’ singer, 45, showed off her incredibly toned figure in a sheer Zuhair Murad jumpsuit and matching cape. Marc, 46, donned black pants, a white shirt and jacket and a pair of sunglasses as he walked the red carpet with his new wife, model Shannon de Lima, 27.

Jennifer at the Billboard Latin Music Awards.

Following her highly publicized breakup with actor Ben Affleck, Jennifer began dating longtime friend Marc in January 2004 and they later married that June. The couple welcomed twins, Max and Emme, 7, in February 2008 before announcing their split in April 2012.

Since then, the actress has been on and off dating dancer Casper Smart, 28, since 2011. The salsa singer has similarly moved on with Shannon, whom he wed last November.

Marc with Shannon on Thursday, April 30, 2015.

Despite their decision to separate, Jennifer has previously stated she and Marc remain close and are dedicated co-parents to their two children. “Marc and I are very good friends, we’re very supportive,” she said during a 2014 interview with ‘HuffPost Live.’
