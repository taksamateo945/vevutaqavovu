Dylan Brosnan, Jack Kilmer Model for Saint Laurent: Photos

February 19, 2024
blog

Hello, Hollywood royalty!

For the latest Saint Laurent men’s campaign, the famed French fashion house decided against using an impossibly handsome male model—in favor of two equally handsome Hollywood up-and-comers!

Enter Dylan Brosnan, the dapper son of former James Bond, Pierce Brosnan. The 17-year-old film student and musician was announced as the brand’s latest face on Tuesday, Nov. 18. Brosnan was reportedly street-cast by YSL’s creative director, Hedi Slimane, this past August in Malibu.

Flaunting the genes he inherited, Brosnan posed for the house — adored by stars like Angelina Jolie and Emma Watson — for a series of photos in which he worked a number of looks, including a tux, a moto jacket, a cardigan, and a Breton-striped T-shirt.

Also announced as one of the faces of Saint Laurent this season is Jack Kilmer, son of the famed Top Gun actor, Val Kilmer. Kilmer, 19, is following in his famous pop’s footsteps as an aspiring actor. However, he’s a bit of a professional model, too, walking in the brand’s spring 2015 runway this past September.

And his print modeling skills aren’t too shabby either! Like Brosnan, Kilmer mugged it in a number of looks: a suit, a leather jacket, a dotted button-up, and a chambray shirt.
