Jennifer Lopez Shows Sexy Legs in a Black Dress: MTV Movie Awards 2015

July 28, 2024
blog

Smoking hot — as always!

Per her usual routine of slaying red carpets far and wide, Jennifer Lopez had another fashion hit at the MTV Movie Awards 2015 on Sunday, April 12, in L.A.

The American Idol judge, 45, struck a fierce pose on the red carpet, wearing a sexy spin on the sophisticated tuxedo. Lopez's risque Anthony Vaccarello x Versus Versace ensemble featured a thigh-grazing miniskirt and blazer paired with a belt, which she accessorized with sky-high Giuseppe Zanotti sandals and a Salvatore Ferragamo metallic clutch. Jewels by Vita Fede, Effy, Melinda Maria, and Melissa Kaye added sparkle. 

Lopez didn't only come to the show to slay on the red carpet, the actress took home the award for Best Scared-As-S–t Performance for her role in The Boy Next Door, which she spoke about with Kelly Osbourne during the pre-show.
