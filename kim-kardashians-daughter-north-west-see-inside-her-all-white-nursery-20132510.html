Kim Kardashian Daughter North West Nursery: See Inside!

May 17, 2024
blog

When Kim Kardashian welcomed daughter North West five weeks premature on June 15, the reality star and her now-fiance Kanye West weren’t the only ones caught off guard. Mom Kris Jenner and sisters Kourtney and Khloe Kardashian went into a frenzy after Kim headed to the hospital to finish North’s nursery in time! In a sneak peek from the Sunday, Oct. 27 episode of E!’s Keeping Up With the Kardashians, Jenner makes her best effort to work as efficiently as possible preparing the all-white room, but the space falls far short of Kourtney’s expectations.

“If you walked home and you were coming in here with the baby, would you want to want to go ‘Oh, look at this steamer . . . look at this table in the middle of the room,'” Kourtney tells her mother, pointing out the work-in-progress room’s flaws. “You’re putting the changing table right under the air conditioning?!”

Agitated, Jenner — who invited Kim, her future son-in-law and granddaughter to live in her Calabasas, Calif. mansion — implores Kourtney to lend a hand instead of lending criticism. “Then move it!” she snaps, before Kourtney calls North’s nursery “despicable.” “We didn’t even have a changing table until 8 o’clock last night,” Jenner explains. This crib appeared in the middle of the room. It was me who had that bed you’re sitting on delivered yesterday. And I found these lucite tables . . . You guys are crazy.”

Despite the mad dash to finalize North’s nursery, the impeccably designed space is warm and inviting — and entirely fit for Kim’s tiny princess.

Decked out in all-white with a $3,950 NurseryWorks Vetro Lucite crib and plush stuffed animals, the nursery — with a closet stocked with baby designer duds from Lanvin and Hermes — has proven to be a family hangout for the new parents and their bundle of joy. “Kanye, Kim and North always take naps together,” a Kardashian source tells Us Weekly. “The three of them cuddle and just hang out.”

Keeping Up With the Kardashians airs Sunday at 9 p.m. (EST) on E!
